Awesome Experience

We heard this place from a friend and we decided to stay for a night before catching our flight the next day. It was a very convenient location for us since we also rented a car. Its 10 mins drive to seminyak, 5-10 mins drive to legian, and they got a lot of souvenir shops nearby. Both sides of the hotel also have convenience stores. The outside interior looks gives us a doubt at first, however once inside, it looks like proper city-hotel. The checked in time could be faster (we waited around 10 mins to be checked in), but I end up chatting with one of the staff the entire time. So the service was great and friendly overall. Will definitely stay here again on our next trip.
